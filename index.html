✦ Founder's Wisdom
From the Founder · On Cutting
Protein is non-negotiable when cutting
When I went from 240lbs to sub-10% body fat, I never dropped protein below 1g per pound of bodyweight. People cut calories but lose muscle and end up just looking smaller. Keep protein high, drop carbs and fat moderately, and trust the process.
From the Founder · On Bulking
A lean bulk is patience, not pizza
The biggest bulking mistake is eating everything in sight. A true lean bulk is 200–300 calories above maintenance max. I learned this the hard way after bulking dirty and spending months cutting unnecessary fat. Slow and steady wins the physique.
From the Founder · On Carb Timing
Carbs around your workout — period
Pre-workout carbs are your fuel. Post-workout carbs are your recovery. I always front-loaded carbs around training and kept them lower at night. This one shift changed my body composition more than anything else I did.
